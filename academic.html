The School is affiliated to the Indian School of Certificate Examination (ICSE) Board.

The School is structured at two levels:
  • The Preparatory School – Classes II to VII
  • The Senior School – Classes VIII to XII.
The Subjects offered at the primary level are:
  • English
  • Hindi
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social sciences.
In class VI the students have the option to choose any one of the Indian languages:
  • Hindi
  • Urdu
  • Punjabi
They are further given the option of a foreign language and can choose between:
  • French
  • German.

However it is compulsory to study English as English is the medium of instruction.
